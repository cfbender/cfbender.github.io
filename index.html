About Me

Scientist

Graduating with a Bachelors in Chemistry from Appalachian State University, and a Masters in Organic Chemistry from the University of Utah, I am trained as an organic chemist and the scientific mindset has become a part of how I solve problems on a day-to-day basis. After learning that the daily work of a chemist wasn't for me, I found software development.

Maker

I am driven by the urge to create. One of the most satisfying feelings is creating something tangible, and being able to show it off to the world. I believe one of the best parts of that is doing things the right way - and being able to be proud of every little step you took. Programming has "scratched that itch" for me in a way that was exactly what I needed as a career, and I enjoy learning more about it every day. In my spare time, I am involved in the local mechanical keyboard community, where I have designed and produced several projects that involve PCB design, as well as case design and manufacturing using 3D printing.
